Bolognese - Ragusea style

Description

This is a recipe I have donde a lot of times, specially when I know I'll have a busy week ahead.

The key is to let the ingredients know each other for a long time. Prep time is short and easy, and most importantly very satisfying!

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 big carrot
  • 1 big red onion
  • 90ml tomato paste
  • 4 small cans of tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • White wine (optional)
  • Cream (optional)
  • Spices:
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Paprika
    • Oregano

Steps

  1. In a big hot pot, add some olive oil and when it smokes add the ground meats combined, leave like that for 3-5 minutes as the meat browns and the start stirring a lot to break apart the meat.
  2. In a food processor or in a grinder, cut the onion and the carrot. Put some olive oil in another nonstick pan and cook the vegetables until they are translucent.
  3. Once the meat has evaporated all its water and the vegetables are cooked, add the vegetables to the big pot and add your tomato paste and stir stir stir.
  4. Before the tomato paste begind to burn add all your canned tomatoes and keep stirring.
  5. Add 2 cups of water or white wine
  6. Leave it cooking on a soft boil for an hour and stirring ocasionally maing sure nothing sticks at the bottom.
  7. Add all of your spices tasting constantly to make sure you got the flavor right and leave it cooking for another hour.
  8. Check the ratio between spolids and liquids and you cand decide if it needs more boiling time or that's the texture you're looking for.
  9. Finally cook some pasta and add as much bolognese sauce as you'd like.
  10. You can add cream when serving for increased texture and flavor.
